ICH Atlas

This external website to the project ICH Atlas contains all the results and visualisation of the Quality Research conducted by the Trama Research Group of Politecnico di Milano Politecnico di Milano regarding the mapping of design and heritage projects on intangible cultural heritage.

The objective of this mapping is to define for Cultural Creative Industries, Educators, Policy Makers and Heritage Institutions best practices to follow and establish directive, so to support the industry and valorisation processes of intangible heritage.
