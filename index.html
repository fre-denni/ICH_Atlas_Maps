ICH Atlas visualisations

Discover the results of the quality research of the projectICH Atlas, through a dynamic and interactive visualisation space that displays the mapping of various projects involved in the valorisation of intangible cultural heritage, showing how the data evolved and its narrated, the professions and resources involved. This research has been conducted by the Trama Research Group ofPolitecnico di Milano.

Using web semantics and graphic visualisation, the visualisation here hosted proposes visual and conceptual ways of navigation through the database of the chosen 49 case studies collected, to show the comparisions that determined the research outcomes. Each of the various maps listed below show different approaches and comparisions of the actors involved, and also offers the possibility for you to make your own comparisions.
